About KYLO

KYLO

The Korean Youth Leadership Organization (KYLO) is the organization behind Korean Youth Online Model United Nations (KYOMUN). KYLO believes in leadership development through MUN participation. Recalling their initial experiences in MUN, KYLO members recognized the challenges faced by beginner delegates, who may find conferences intimidating and competitive. In response, KYLO established a conference aimed at beginner delegates, guiding them towards effective MUN participation.

Beyond MUN, KYLO strives to encourage community engagement among students. As part of this commitment, profits from KYOMUN will be used to support a fundraising initiative that provides medical supplies to a hospital.
