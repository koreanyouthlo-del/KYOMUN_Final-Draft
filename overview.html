KYOMUN Overview

KYOMUN Benefits

Quality MUN Experience

KYOMUN is a highly accessible MUN conference hosted by award-winning delegates and experienced chairs.

Beginner-Friendly Experience

KYOMUN will host an educational session to teach new delegates about basic MUN procedures and tips to succeed as delegates. Furthermore, individual feedback will be provided for delegates.

Networking Experience

Delegates will have the opportunity to connect with fellow delegates, chairs, and Secretariat members to form connections lasting beyond the conference.
