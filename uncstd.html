UNCSTD Introduction

The United Nations Commission on Science and Technology for Development (UNCSTD) is a subsidiary body of the Economic and Social Council (ECOSOC). The UNCSTD has advised the international community on the role of science, technology, and innovation in sustainable development. The UNCSTD has cooperated with the United Nations General Assembly (UNGA) and ECOSOC, providing essential assistance regarding developments in science and technology. The representatives of the UNCSTD examine emerging technologies and assess their potential impact on development goals. To date, the UNCSTD has contributed to discussions on the digital divide and the expansion of information and communication technologies, especially for developing countries. Moreover, in recent years, the UNCSTD has paid extra attention to the potential consequences of increased developments in AI.
