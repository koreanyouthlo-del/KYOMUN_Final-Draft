About KYOMUN

Introduction

The Korean Youth Online Model United Nations (KYOMUN) is an online Model United Nations conference. Hosted by KYLO, our mission is to provide an accessible, beginner-friendly, and meaningful MUN experience that empowers students to develop leadership skills.

All profits will be donated to a medical initiative. Through participation in KYOMUN, delegates not only engage in debate and diplomacy, but also contribute to a cause that extends beyond the conference itself.
